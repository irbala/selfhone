About Selfhone

Welcome to my journey as a beginner web developer. After finding myself engrossed in my "cellphone", I came up with the name "Selfhone" as a combination of "self" and "hone" as a representation of my journey from being captive to my device to actively honing and shaping my skills and abilities. The main idea is conveying the idea of continuous growth and refinement and representing self-improvement along the way. Aiming to encapsulate the transformation and growth as a web developer. This portfolio is a testament to my pursuit of perpetual learning and my creative ardor for web development, where imagination and progress intertwine.
