About Selfhone

Welcome to my journey as a beginner web developer. Aiming to encapsulate the transformation and growth as a web developer after finding myself captive to my cellphone, I came up with the name "Selfhone" by turning the word "cellphone" into a combination of "self" and "hone" representing my journey in actively honing and shaping my skills and abilities. The main concept is conveying the idea of continuous growth and refinement as i'm representing self-improvement along the way.
